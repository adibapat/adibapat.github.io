Ross Alumni Engagement

For my Summer 2023 internship, I worked with the Ross Alumni Engagement office to create a Young Alumni program for graduates 0-10 years out of college. Fully in charge of this project, I interviewed 50+ alumni around the world to gauge their experience with AlumniAdvantage - our flagship alumni program. I conducted in-depth research on peer business institions to understand the differences between our respective programs. Most importantly, I collaborated with different teams across Ross, such as marketing, finance, and alumni relations, to brainstorm feasible solutions. Ultimately, I presented a 5-step plan of how the business school should approach building this program, which received high recognition and praise.

User Experience Design

I conducted a user experience research project to explore the need for a community feature on popular music streaming platforms such as Spotify, Apple Music, and SoundCloud. This feature aims to enhance social interaction by allowing users to share, discuss, and discover music within a community setting. To determine the necessity and potential impact of this feature, I interviewed 10+ fellow U-M students who are active users of these services. I performed thematic analysis, including coding and creating themes from the interview data. I created an affinity diagram from the thematic analysis and concluded that adding a community feature would be a valuable enhancement for these music streaming platforms. This semester-long journey highlights the importance of user-centered design in identifying and addressing unmet needs in digital products.
